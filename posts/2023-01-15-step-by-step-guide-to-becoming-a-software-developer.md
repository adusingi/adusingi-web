I am always surprised by the number of people who are still not aware of available resources to learn almost anything for free. From Web Developer, Artificial Intelligence, Augmented Reality, Blockchain, Data scientist, etc. The contents are of very good quality and some of them are proposed by prestigious universities such as Harvard and Stanford. There are also different types of online help, mentorship, and forums for groups taking the same class, to ask questions, share tips, etc.

Besides that, there are resources dedicated to helping start a new career as a software engineer. The resources have information on creating an online profile, preparing for interviews, doing game-coding, and sharing personal work on a platform like GitHub where recruiters can review.

During my trip to Rwanda, I did a few workshops to share resources that I hope would help newly graduated students to find a remote job. Below, I am sharing a list of links that offer some advice on how to land a remote job or start a new career as a software engineer.

## 2. Education, Education, Education

### My Recommendations

1. **First:** Start learning HTML/CSS
2. **Second:** Learn JavaScript for Front-End
3. **Last:** Learn at least one language for Back-End (Python, Java, PHP, Ruby, etc.)
