# Welcome to My Blog

I'm excited to launch this new space where I'll be sharing my journey as a builder, developer, and explorer based in rural Japan.

## What You'll Find Here

This blog is where I'll document:

- **Tech experiments**: AI, blockchain, web development, and other technical explorations
- **Life in Japan**: Insights from living and working in Okayama, cultural observations, and local discoveries
- **Building digital products**: Lessons learned from creating [Bento Kumiko](https://www.bentokumiko.com), [Ushimado Town](https://www.ushimadotown.com), and other projects
- **Tai-Chi practice** (太極拳): Reflections on martial arts training and discipline

## Why Now?

After years of building products and helping others tell their stories, it's time to share my own. This blog represents a commitment to documenting what I'm learning, creating, and discovering.

## What's Next?

I'm working on several exciting projects and have plenty of ideas to explore. Stay tuned for posts about:

- Setting up a minimal blog system with Vite and TypeScript
- Travel planning strategies for Japan
- Code experiments and technical deep-dives
- Team-building retreats in rural Japan

Thanks for reading, and welcome aboard!
